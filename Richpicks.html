2. Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) Intraday Trading Strategy

MACD is most notably used by technical analysts. This strategy is most commonly used to identify the direction of the trend, its rate of change as well as its velocity. Traders with this strategy can predict when the changes in a trend are about to take place. Most occasionally it is stated to be a lagging indicator. However, it is wholly up to the trader to use this as part of his day-to-day toolset.

The next strategy is among one of the favorite strategies of cryptocurrency traders.

3. Parabolic Stop And Reverse (SAR) Intraday Trading Strategy

This popular strategy makes it easy for traders to see where to place a stop-loss order. After that, it allows traders to use the tool to set trailing stops as the price moves up in a bullish direction. Parabolic SAR is convenient to use. It also offers a very easy-to-understand visual signal system. The common practice of this strategy is to find the potential reversals as well as gauge trend strength. It is often amalgamated with other indicators, to confirm the signals beforehand taking any position size.

It is always a good practice to combine different day trading strategies if you want the desired results. The upcoming strategy, namely Bollinger Bands Intraday Trading Strategy, can also be used effectively and efficiently by being integrated with other strategies. As the strategy works on the graphical representation of bars and bands, you will get an overview of the conditions of assets that are oversold or overbought. Let’s get into the details of Bollinger Bands’ strategy.

4. Bollinger Bands Intraday Trading Strategy

This remarkable trading strategy is used to regulate the entry and exit points for a trade. The bands are used to discover the conditions of overbought or oversold. When the bands get tight, it is a signal that volatility has dropped and an explosive move is anticipated in the trade. It is considered risky to use only the bands to carry out a trade, since the focus of the indicator is only on the price and volatility. Combining with other strategies is advised.

Next up is the trading strategy that will use both fast-moving and slow-moving averages.

5. Moving Averages Intraday Trading Strategy

In this strategy, a fast-moving and a slow-moving average line are combined. They can act as a signal to take the position when the price passes through both of the moving averages. These are technical and based on mathematical formulas that are designed to form visual
representations. This way, traders can conveniently analyze individual data points through a series of timestamps.

Conclusion

Those were some insights into major intraday trading strategies that are vital enough for you to learn, in order to become a good day trader.
